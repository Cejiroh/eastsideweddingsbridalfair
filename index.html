Our Objectives

Affordable Participation, Economic Growth, and Tourism Promotion

Lowest Participation Fee

Exhibitors can join for just P 10,000 P 15,000, with hassle-free booth designs provided.

Economic Growth

By hosting the fair twice a year, we aim to boost business for small and midsize enterprises in Rizal.

Promote Rizal Tourism

Attract couples from Metro Manila to discover the diverse and skilled wedding industry in Rizal.

Food Tasting

Join us for an unforgettable culinary experience at Densols Caterings grand food tasting event! This exclusive event is open to all pre-registered attendees and offers a unique opportunity to sample a wide array of delectable dishes crafted by our expert chefs.

Indulge in a variety of flavors, from savory appetizers to mouth-watering main courses and delightful desserts. Whether youre planning a wedding, corporate event, or any special occasion, this tasting event will give you a taste of the exceptional quality and creativity that Densols Catering brings to every event.

Free Entrance

Unlike other bridal fairs, our event is free for all walk-ins and pre-registered couples, making it inclusive for everyone.

Target Market

Connecting Rizal and Metro Manila

We aim to attract 50% of our clients from Rizal and 50% from Metro Manila and adjacent areas. Rediscover Rizal as a premier wedding destination, offering a variety of options based on vibe, price, and view.
